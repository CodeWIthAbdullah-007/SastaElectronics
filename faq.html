FAQ

Find answers to common questions

How do I place an order?
Browse products, add to cart, proceed to checkout. Fill shipping details, choose payment, and confirm. You'll receive confirmation via email and SMS.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), Bank Transfer, and Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard). All card payments are processed through secure, encrypted gateways.
What is your delivery time?
Karachi: 1-2 business days. Other cities: 3-5 business days. Remote areas: up to 7 business days. You'll receive tracking info once shipped.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! Free shipping on orders above Rs. 5,000. Below that, a flat Rs. 250 is charged regardless of location.
What is your return policy?
7-day hassle-free return policy. Defective or damaged products can be returned within 7 days for replacement or refund. Products must be in original packaging.
Are products genuine with warranty?
All products are 100% genuine with official manufacturer warranty. We source directly from authorized distributors.
How can I track my order?
Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email. Also track from "My Orders" in your dashboard.
